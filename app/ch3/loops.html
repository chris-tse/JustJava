After Chapter 2, we can now direct how our program will execute depending on certain parameters. Those are commonly used in every type of program.

Now we will move on to loops, which is the way we tell the program to do something repeatedly over and over while a certain condition is true. In Java there are three main ways to write loops, which is what we will be covering in Chapter 3.

The three loop structures we will be looking at are the for loop, while loop, and do while loops.
